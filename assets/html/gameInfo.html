How to Play

Pick Your Candidate:

Choose between Chad or Barry before the game begins. The unselected character will auto-play as your rival.

Catch the Votes:

Click mouse or press 'm' key to open your character's mouth and collect falling items to score points. Press 'Spacebar' key or the 'Gear ⚙' icon to pause game and see the game settings.

Avoid Onions 🧅:

Onions are trouble! Eating one will disable your character for 3.5 seconds.

Points:

  • Burger 🍔: 20
  • Pizza 🍕: 10
  • Hot dog 🌭: 8
  • Chips 🍟: 5
  • Cookies 🍪: 3
  • Onion 🧅: 0

Difficulty Levels

There are 3 difficulty levels, Beginner, Intermeidate and Expert. In intermeidate and expert levels the opponent or robot gets smarter and takes less time to wake up after the onion trap.

Win the States 🗺️

Earn enough points to light up U.S. states on the map.

The first to reach 270 points wins the game!

Leaderboard Rules 🏆

Only winners make the leaderboard.

Rankings are based on the most states won.

If tied, the opponent's points decide the champion!
